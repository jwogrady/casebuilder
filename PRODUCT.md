# Product Brief

## One sentence

CaseBuilder turns court records into verified case knowledge that attorneys review before generating filings.

## Product category

CaseBuilder is a litigation operating system.

It is not a chatbot, document generator, or isolated legal research tool. It is the workspace where the factual record is captured, structured, reviewed, and converted into work product.

## Core promise

Evidence before opinion. Understanding before drafting. Review before generation.

## Users

- Self-represented litigants who need disciplined case organization.
- Attorneys who need fast reconstruction of complex records.
- Paralegals who need traceable evidence workspaces.
- Legal operations teams building repeatable litigation workflows.

## Primary workflow

1. Capture source material.
2. Build the Canonical Case Record.
3. Review case understanding.
4. Approve or revise understanding.
5. Generate work product from approved understanding.
6. File.
7. Capture the new filing and repeat.

## Non-goals

- Replace attorney judgment.
- Generate filings from unreviewed analysis.
- Treat a database as the source of truth.
- Hide provenance behind opaque summaries.
- Collapse capture, analysis, and drafting into one uncontrolled step.

## Product standard

A CaseBuilder artifact is acceptable only if a user can answer:

- Where did this fact come from?
- What page supports it?
- What version of the record was used?
- Was the understanding reviewed?
- What changed since the last iteration?
